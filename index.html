Essentials

A quick guide to the in-condo features and building amenities guests tend to use most.

Living Room
Home Theater

The living room is set up as a home theater with a Sonos Beam Gen 2, Sonos Mini Sub, two Play:1 speakers, and a Roku Ultra in guest mode for a superb TV, movie, and music experience. Please keep the volume considerate for the neighbors, especially after 10:00 PM or before 8:00 AM.

TV
Roku Players

Each TV is equipped with a Roku for easy access to streaming apps.

You are welcome to sign into your own streaming services during your stay. For privacy, our Roku players are in guest mode, which will automatically clear login information after your checkout date. Simply enter your checkout date when prompted and sign in to your apps as usual.

Kitchen
Coffee And Tea Station

Includes drip coffee, filters, ground coffee, Nespresso pods including decaf, tea, and an electric kettle.

Building
Rooftop And Shared Amenities
  • Rooftop pool that is shallow and best for soaking, cooling off, and relaxed family time
  • Plenty of loungers, shaded seating areas, and comfortable chairs
  • Outdoor grill for easy cooking upstairs
  • Two jacuzzis: one saltwater and one standard chlorine
  • Small but very usable gym
  • 5th-floor lap pool with posted hours that may alternate between lap swimming and general use
