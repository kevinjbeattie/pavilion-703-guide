Know before you go

A few practical notes go a long way in Puerto Vallarta, especially for first-time visitors staying in Zona Romántica.

Local tips
Easy day-one advice
Very walkable
Zona Romántica is easy to explore on foot, and many guests will not need a car at all.
Cobblestones & sidewalks
Street surfaces can be uneven and sidewalks narrow, so comfortable shoes are a smart call.
Cash helps
Cards are widely accepted, but pesos are useful for small shops, taxis, tips, and casual food stops.
Neighborhood rhythm
What guests should expect
Street food can be great
Some of the best casual meals are neighborhood taco stands and market stops, especially in the evening.
Nightlife runs late
This area can stay lively into the night, so light sleepers may appreciate earplugs.
Local charm is real
Puerto Vallarta blends polished hospitality with a lived-in neighborhood feel, which is part of what makes it special.
